Company Logo
PNG or JPG · Max 1MB · Landscape (wide) logos work best in the header
Logo Guidelines
File type: PNG or JPG. PNG is preferred for logos with a transparent background.
File size: Maximum 1MB.
Shape: Landscape (wider than tall) logos work best. A square or portrait logo will be scaled down to fit the header height, which can make it appear small.
If it looks too wide or distorted: Crop your logo to around 400×120px then re-upload.
Background: A transparent PNG looks most professional against your chosen header colour.
Resolution: Aim for at least 400px wide at 150dpi or higher.
